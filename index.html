Why Issy's Guilt-Free Treats?

Issy's Guilt-Free Treats are for anyone who has to avoid the "naughty" foods but still wants to indulge in a treat once in a while. A treat should be something indulgent and delicious you can enjoy without feeling guilty! That's how Issy's Guilt-Free Treats came about.

After years of being frustrated by the Free-From sections of supermarkets either, not having suitable indulgent treats or, packing any gluten free treats with extra sugar, I decided to develop my own range. All my treats are completely:

  • Gluten free
  • Dairy free
  • Refined sugar free
  • Suitable for vegans
  • Suitable for vegetarians

Plus (to make them even better for you).... There's none of the added junk!

  • No preservatives
  • No bulking agents
  • No additives

JUST pure natural ingredients!

Who are they perfect for?

If you have a child who has to avoid the above or you are just trying to keep them away from the sugar aisle, these could be perfect for them. They're a great Christmas stocking filler, Easter basket addition and birthday pressie!

If you have a girlfriend, boyfriend, mum, dad, sister, uncle, best friend etc trying to keep healthy but who keeps getting tempted by the chocolate buttons or Ferrero Rocher these could be the perfect gift!

Take a look through the range, see what takes your fancy, then place your order and enjoy to your heart's desire!

(The best bit is that you don't have to feel guilty about any of it!)

About Issy

Unfortunately at the age of 17 I had to give up gluten, dairy, eggs and refined sugar. Although I was thrilled I got better and my health issues were resolved I was not thrilled at the lack of treats I could enjoy. Easter was not the same, no matter how many packets of seeds or 100% dark chocolate bars my mum filled my Easter basket with, it never stopped me looking longingly at my sisters Lindt Easter Egg and chocolate truffles. Christmas was always awkward. While my friends could exchange edible goodies everyone struggled to think of a little edible gift they could easily get for me. Don't even get me started on Valentines Day! Getting me a box of chocolates was never an option for my boyfriend. So I wanted to make yummy treats that I and anyone else out there like me, could truly enjoy and indulge in! To start with my little balls of heaven are on sale. However, the range is only going to expand, think gooey brownies, crumbly apple crumble and fluffy light muffins. So keep an eye on this page and give my treats a go!
